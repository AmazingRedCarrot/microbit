# Neopixel

Extension is available at https://makecode.microbit.org/pkg/microsoft/pxt-neopixel.

Pin P15 is used to control the 4 Neopixel RGB LEDs on the micro:Maqueen with the "RGB (GRB format)".

RED | GREEN | BLUE | RESULT
--- | ----- | ---- | ------
0 | 0 | 255 | Blue
0 | 255 | 0 | Green
255 | 0 | 0 | Red
255 | 255 | 0 | Yellow
255 | 0 | 255 | Magenta
0 | 255 | 255 | Cyan
255 | 255 | 255 | White

# micro:Maqueen

## Pinout
PIN | USE
--- | ---
P0 | Buzzer
P1 | Extension
P2 | Extension
P8 | LED-L
P12 | LED-R
P13 | Line-L
P14 | Line-R
P15 | RGB Ambient Light (16 million colors) x 4
P16 | Infrared Receiver (NEC decoder) 

## Servo Motors
M1 | M2 | DIRECTION
-- | -- | ---------
CW | CW | Forward
CW | CCW | Right
CCW | CW | Left
CCW | CCW | Backward
